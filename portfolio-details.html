Educational Consultancy Platform

12 Minds Plus Educational Consultancy is a professional website designed to help students pursue international education opportunities. The platform provides comprehensive services including university admissions assistance, visa guidance, and career counseling.

Key Features:

  • University admissions support and guidance
  • Visa assistance and documentation help
  • Career counseling services
  • Bilingual support (English & Amharic)
  • Student consultation booking system
  • Resource library and educational materials
  • Success stories and testimonials
  • Team showcase and contact information

The website serves as a gateway for students seeking to study abroad, offering professional guidance and support throughout their educational journey. Built with modern web technologies and responsive design to ensure accessibility across all devices.
